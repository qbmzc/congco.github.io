使用curl请求websocket

<!-- more -->

```bash
curl --no-buffer -H 'Connection: keep-alive, Upgrade' -H 'Upgrade: websocket' -v -H 'Sec-WebSocket-Version: 13' -H 'Sec-WebSocket-Key: websocket' http://localhost:8080/websocket/pod/itaimei/hls-service ws | od -t c 1
```
